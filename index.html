HAIM The Band

About

Sisters Este, Daniella and Alana Haim came together to form the rock group HAIM in 2007. From the "The Wire" to "Right Now," HAIM's lyrics express all the thoughts and feelings of love in a casual yet moody way. Their newest album, Women in Music Pt. III, came out in July and features the profound single "I Know Alone." At a time where everyone seems to be alone, this song reminds me we are in fact not alone in this weird stage of life.
